## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard for customer segmentation analysis. It categorizes customers based on behavior and engagement to identify high-value segments and understand overall customer distribution.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Segment customers based on behavior and engagement  
- Identify high-value and low-value customer groups  
- Analyze distribution across different segments  
- Support targeted marketing and retention strategies  

---

## ⚙️ Dashboard Features
- Customer segmentation breakdown  
- Distribution of customers across segments  
- Visual comparison of engagement levels  
- Interactive filters for deeper analysis  
- Clean and intuitive dashboard design  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI  
- Data Modeling  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- A small percentage of customers belong to high-value segments  
- Majority of customers fall into moderate or low engagement groups  
- Customer contribution is uneven across segments  
- High-value customers drive a significant portion of overall performance  

---

## 🚀 Project Outcome
Developed a segmentation dashboard that transforms customer data into **actionable insights**, helping businesses improve targeting, retention, and customer engagement strategies.
